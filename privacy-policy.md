
Privacy Policy — 20 Arcade Club
Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview
20 Arcade Club is a collection of 20 classic arcade games. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect
Data stored locally on your device
The app stores the following data only on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party:

Player profiles (name, chosen avatar emoji)
Game scores and personal bests for each game
Game settings (sound on/off)
In-progress game saves (so you can resume where you left off)
Data we do NOT collect
We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information
We do not create user accounts
We do not track your location
We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
We do not share any data with third parties
Third-Party Services
The app connects to the following external services solely to retrieve game content:

Trivia Quiz — opentdb.com
What it does: Fetches trivia questions for the Trivia Quiz game
Website: https://opentdb.com
Data sent: Only a request for questions (no user data)
Their privacy policy: https://opentdb.com/privacypolicy.php
Trivia Quiz (German) — api.opentrivia.de
What it does: Fetches German-language trivia questions
Website: https://opentrivia.de
Data sent: Only a request for questions (no user data)
Wikipedia REST API — wikipedia.org
What it does: Fetches word clues for the Crossword game
Website: https://www.wikipedia.org
Data sent: Only a request for a specific article summary (no user data)
Their privacy policy: https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Policy:Privacy_policy
All external requests are fetch-only. No user data, device identifiers, or personal information is included in any request.

Children's Privacy
20 Arcade Club does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social features. All data remains local to the device.

Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no account to delete and no data on our servers.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy when new features are added. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
