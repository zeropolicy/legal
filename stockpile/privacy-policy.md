# Privacy Policy — Stockpile

**Last updated: June 2026**

## Overview

Stockpile is a simple, offline inventory app for your home or small office — scan, organize
by category & location, track quantities, and never run out; no ads, no account, all data on
your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the
app handles, how it is used, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Data stored locally on your device

The app stores the following **only on your device** using local storage. This data never
leaves your device and is never sent to our servers (we have none) or shared with any third
party:

- Your inventory items (names, quantities, units, prices, notes, expiry dates, barcodes)
- Photos you take or attach to items
- Categories and locations you create
- Item quantity history (your add/remove changes)
- App settings (e.g. which fields are shown, theme, language)

### Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information
- We do not create user accounts
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

The app connects to **one** external service, and only when **you choose** to look up a
scanned barcode. The request is **fetch-only** and includes **no user data, device
identifiers, or personal information**:

- **OpenFoodFacts** — an open, community product database. When you scan a barcode and the app
  looks it up, it sends **only the scanned barcode number** (a product identifier, not personal
  data) to retrieve public product details (name, brand, image, ingredients) to help you fill
  in the item. Nothing about you or your device is sent.
  - Website: <https://world.openfoodfacts.org>
  - Their privacy policy: <https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy>
  - Data sent by Stockpile: only a content request (the barcode); no user data.

If you never use the barcode lookup, the app makes no network requests and works fully
offline. Photos you add are stored only on your device and are never uploaded.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — used to scan barcodes and to take photos of your items. Images stay on your
  device.
- **Photos / media (optional)** — only if you choose to attach a photo from your library.
- **Internet** — used only for the optional OpenFoodFacts barcode lookup described above.

## Children's Privacy

Stockpile does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app contains no
advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social features. All personal inventory data remains
local to the device.

## Data Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time from
within the app (Settings → Reset all data) or by uninstalling the app. There is no account to
delete and no data on our servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when new features are added. Changes will be reflected by updating
the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance
of the updated policy.
