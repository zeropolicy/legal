# Privacy Policy — TeamRoomie

**Last updated: June 2026**

## Overview

TeamRoomie is a complete organizer for a shared flat (a WG / Wohngemeinschaft) —
shared calendar, chores rota, shopping & to-do lists, a shared-expenses Kasse,
repairs, borrowing, the fridge, rooms, house rules and a private Lead area —
built on a **local-first** principle: all of your flatshare's data stays on the
roomies' own devices. There are no central servers and no cloud account. This
policy explains what data the app handles, how it stays on your devices, and your
rights.

**In one line: nothing about your WG leaves your phones. There is no server to
send it to.**

## Data We Store (only on your devices)

TeamRoomie stores the following **only on your device**, in encrypted local
storage (AES-256). This data is never sent to us, because we operate no servers:

- Roomie profiles (names, roles, colors)
- Calendar events, appointments and house meetings
- To-dos, the rotating cleaning board (Putzplan), shopping & custom lists
- The shared-money **WG-Kasse**: expenses and settle-ups, payment details,
  payment records/receipts, repairs (incl. on-device repair photos) and
  recurring shared bills
- Borrowing (who lent/borrowed what), the fridge planner, and room assignments
- House rules (Hausordnung) and votes
- In the **Lead Zone** (separately encrypted, unlocked by biometrics/PIN, and
  **never synced to a roomie's device**): the lease record (Mietvertrag), the key
  register, per-roomie rent, private notes, and the document/password vault

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information on any server
- We do not create user accounts — there is nothing to sign up for
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or fingerprinting SDKs
- We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties

## How Synchronization Works (no cloud)

When more than one roomie uses the app, TeamRoomie synchronizes data **directly
between your own devices**:

- **At home:** over your local Wi-Fi network, device-to-device. The data never
  reaches the internet.
- **On the go:** only when *you* choose to export an **AES-256-encrypted** file
  and share it through your own channel (email, messenger). Nothing is exported
  automatically and nothing passes through our infrastructure.

The Lead Zone (lease, key register, private notes, vault) is **never** included in
any sync — it stays on the Lead Roomie's device only. We never see, store, or
relay any of this data.

## Pricing

TeamRoomie is **free** — every feature is available to everyone at no cost. There
are **no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no advertising**. Because we run
no servers and collect no data, there is nothing to monetise: the app is free to
use, period.

## Children's Privacy

TeamRoomie is intended for adults sharing a home. It contains **no advertising, no
third-party tracking, and no social features**, and it does not knowingly collect
any data from children under 13. All data remains local to the roomies' devices.

## Data Security & Deletion

All data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and the encryption keys are held in
your device's secure hardware keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). The Lead
Zone is encrypted in a separate store unlocked only by biometrics or PIN. Because
everything is local, you can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app.
There is no account to delete and no data on our servers.

## A note on the Kasse and contract features

The WG-Kasse, payment receipts and the lease (Mietvertrag) features are **internal
book-keeping conveniences only** — they have no legal or accounting standing and
are not a substitute for formal records or professional advice.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when new features are added. Changes are reflected by
updating the "Last updated" date above.
